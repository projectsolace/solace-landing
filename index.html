Our Vision for Solace

Studies have shown that writing or speaking what’s on our minds is extremely beneficial to our sense of self and well-being. Unfortunately many people don't take the time to write out what's on their minds and others are often embarrassed or unwilling to express themselves in front of another person. This is the problem we are trying to solve.

Solace is a platform that provides users with an easy and accurate method of self-introspection. Each day, users are encouraged to share their thoughts and feelings with Solace by simply speaking to the app. Solace will then analyze the recordings and output a visual representation of the user's personality and emotional insights, allowing users to learn and discover more about themselves.

Mobile

Solace is an iOS application built using React Native. It harnesses the power of IBM Watson’s supercomputer to help users track their mental and emotional processes over time.

The way it works is simple. Log in or sign up...

...enjoy the beautiful views and inspirational message, hit the big red microphone button, and express yourself.

Users are encouraged to speak for at least one minute for Watson to generate an accurate analysis.

Watson sends back over 50 personality and emotional insights based on the recording.

To view their data, users can go to Charts and choose to filter data by week, month, or all time. Users can also see their total data to track how their recordings have changed over time.

Web

We wanted to add a functionality that allows administrators of the app to analyze data based on demographics.

Thus, we built a web portal that aggregates all data and allows admins to understand general trends among different demographic categories.

Chrome Extension

A bonus feature we implemented is a Chrome extension that makes it easy to interact with Solace when it is inconvenient to talk on your phone.

If a thought suddenly pops up while you are browsing on your computer, you can immediately click on the extension and type away.

Once finished, your account will be automatically updated with the latest analysis ready for you to view.
